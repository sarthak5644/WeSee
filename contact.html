More About Me.

Hello! We are the team behind WeSee. Our members — Pooja Thorat, Rutik Tupe, and Sarthak Velapure — are final-year students pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at AISSMS Institute of Information Technology, Pune.

As aspiring technology enthusiasts, we have always been passionate about Artificial Intelligence. Throughout our academic journey at AISSMS Institute of Information Technology, Pune, we have explored diverse domains of AI and Machine Learning, continuously enhancing our skills and applying them to real-world challenges.

One of the most significant highlights of our academic career is our final year project – WeSee, where we are developing an innovative solution to empower the visually impaired community. This project not only challenges us to combine our technical knowledge and creativity but also allows us to contribute meaningfully toward building a more accessible and inclusive world.

We are truly excited about the possibilities that WeSee brings and look forward to sharing our progress and outcomes with you.
